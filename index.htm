E-waste, short for electronic waste, refers to discarded electronic devices, equipment, and gadgets that have reached the end of their useful life. These can include old computers, mobile phones, televisions, printers, household appliances, and other electronic devices. E-waste encompasses anything powered by electricity or batteries that is no longer wanted or functional. Improper disposal of e-waste can be harmful to both the environment and human health due to the toxic materials these electronics contain, such as lead, mercury, cadmium, and other hazardous substances.
